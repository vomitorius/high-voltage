Use a FTP manager and upload these files on your server:
- guestbook.php ("book" folder)
- guestbook.txt ("book" folder)
- GuestBook.html
- GuestBook.swf

After that,send these commands:
 chmod 755 guestbook.php
 chmod 777 guestbook.txt

Your GuestBook is ready for visitors.
Enjoy !

Important note:
In order to be able to test this GuestBook OffLine,
PHP 4.xx and IIS / PWS are required !
